Gamma is the acceleration of delta.
$$ \frac{\partial^2 V}{\partial S^2} =\frac{ e^{-qT}N'(d_1)}{\sigma S\sqrt{T}}
$$

where $$ N'(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-x^2/2} $$

(see [Black-Scholes-Merton formula](../methods/black-scholes-merton.html))
