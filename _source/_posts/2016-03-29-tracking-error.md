The tracking error of a portfolio is defined as the annualized standard
deviation of its excess returns over a benchmark.

$$ \alpha =( r_{Pf} - r_{Bm}) $$

$$ TE = sqrt{\sum{(\alpha_i - \bar{\alpha})^2}} $$
